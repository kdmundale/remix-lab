City Council

WHat we do

City Council focuses on the long-range vision for the community, establishing policies that affect the overall operation of the city.

Public Comment

Members of the public are encouraged to address council members in person at regular meetings on the second and fourth Tuesdays of the month, at 7:30 p.m., in City Council Chambers, City Hall Annex, 120 N. Duke Street. Please email Bernie Harris, City Clerk, to request to make comment at the next meeting. Please include your name and comments.

Rules for public comment

Meeting Schedule

Regular Meetings

The Council Of The City Of Lancaster holds regularly scheduled meetings on the 2nd & 4th Tuesday of every month (except December 24) in Council Chambers, City Hall Annex (Marion Street entrance), 120 N. Duke Street, Lancaster, PA at 7:30 p.m. (prevailing time).

Committee Meetings

6:00 p.m. in the Commission Room in City Hall Annex, (Marion Street Entrance) 120 N. Duke St. on the first Monday of the month (except September 2).
